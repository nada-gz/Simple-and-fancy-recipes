Caramel Frappucino Recipe

A frappe is the perfect way to cool down on a hot summer day.
Frappuccino is a line of blended iced coffee drinks sold by Starbucks.
It consists of coffee or cr√®me base, blended with ice and ingredients such as flavored syrups and usually topped with whipped cream and or spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ice
  • 1 cup strongly brewed coffee of choice
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup caramel sauce
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar

Directions

  1. Blend the ice, coffee, milk, caramel and sugar together
  2. Pour into large glasses and decorate with caramel drizzle or top off with whipped cream.
